Research Results

H1 - Relative Strength

Status

Completed

Outcome

Rejected

Summary

Relative Strength alone did not produce a statistically significant trading edge.

Findings

- Win rate remained close to random.
- Expectancy remained approximately zero.
- No consistent separation between high and low ranked stocks.
- Relative Strength should not be used as a standalone entry signal.

Decision

Rejected as an individual factor.

Future research should evaluate Relative Strength only as part of a multi-factor strategy.
